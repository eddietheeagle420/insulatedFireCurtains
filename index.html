Firescreen® Temperature (EI)

The Firescreen® Range - Brought to you by Hoefnagels Fire Safety

Firescreen® Temperature fire-resistant roller screens meet the important criteria: flame density to keep the element intact (E), limitation of thermal radiation of up to 1 kW/m2 (W) and thermal insulation of the temperature (I). With these roller screens you can, for example, realise an evacuation route or create an attack route for the fire brigade.

Firescreen® Temperature fire curtains are suitable for sealing off fire compartments and ensure that, at a temperature of 1000°C on the fire side, the rise on the non-fire side is only 140°C. Firescreen Temperature fire-resistant roller screens have an EI classification and are tested according to EN 1634-1 and EN 13501-1.

Optionally, we can supply both the Standard and P versions of this roller screen with a smoke-resistant function, Sa / S200, in accordance with the standard EN 1634-3.

Why Firescreen® Temperature?

  • Standard version: up to 60 minutes of fire-resistance
  • P-version: up to 120 minutes of fire-resistance
  • Heat radiation <1 kW/m²
  • Tested according to standard EN 1634-1
  • Tested according to standard EN 13501-1
  • Little installation space
  • Little side space
  • Lightweight
  • Maximum recess Standard version: 6,000 x 5,000 mm
  • Maximum recess P version: 8,400 x 4,500 mm - Please contact us for deviating sizes.
  • Both the Standard version and the P version are optionally available with smoke-resistant function, in accordance with the Sa / S200 standard: EN 1634-3
